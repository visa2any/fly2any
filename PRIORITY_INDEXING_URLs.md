### **"URL not indexed" after 7 days:**

2. Verify sitemap includes the URL
3. Check for duplicate content issues
4. Re-request indexing

### **"URL submitted but not indexed":**
1. Wait 2-4 weeks (normal processing time)
2. Check page loading speed
3. Verify hreflang tags are correct
4. Ensure unique, valuable content
