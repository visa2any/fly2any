## Summary

**Visual Hierarchy:**
1. Success message (largest, most prominent)
2. Booking reference (important, copyable)
3. Quick actions (easy access)
4. Flight details (primary info)
5. Payment & next steps (supporting info)
6. Tips & FAQs (helpful extras)
7. Support & footer (always available)

**Color Usage:**
- Green = Success, confirmation
- Blue = Departure, primary actions
- Purple = Arrival, secondary
- Amber = Tips, warnings
- Gray = Neutral, structure

**Spacing:**
- Generous whitespace
- Clear sections
- Comfortable reading
- Mobile-friendly

**Typography:**
- Clear hierarchy
- Readable sizes
- Good contrast
- Monospace for codes

**End of Visual Guide**
