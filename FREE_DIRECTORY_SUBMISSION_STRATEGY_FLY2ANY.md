- ❌ **Red Flags:** Automatic approval, broken links, spam content
- ⚠️ **Caution:** Excessive ads, unclear policies, poor design

- Unique descriptions for each directory
- Accurate business information (NAP consistency)
- Professional business imagery

## ⚠️ 10. RISK MITIGATION & BEST PRACTICES

### **Avoiding Penalties**
- Never pay for directory submissions on low-quality sites
- Maintain NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency
- Avoid over-optimization of anchor texts
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Regular audit of directory quality

### **Spam Prevention**
- Space out submissions (max 5-10 per week)
- Use varied descriptions and anchor texts
- Avoid automated submission tools
- Monitor for negative SEO attacks
- Regular cleanup of low-quality links

### **Compliance & Guidelines**
- Follow each directory's specific guidelines
- Respect submission limits and requirements
- Maintain professional communication
- Keep documentation of all submissions
- Regular policy review and compliance
