================================================================================
                         BEFORE vs AFTER EXAMPLE
================================================================================

BEFORE (Non-Semantic):
  <header className="sticky top-0 z-[100]">
    <div className="absolute z-[9999]">Dropdown</div>
  </header>
  <div className="z-[60]">Modal Backdrop</div>

Problems:
  - Unclear purpose of each z-index value
  - No hierarchy or pattern
  - Difficult to maintain or extend
  - Arbitrary values that could conflict

AFTER (Semantic):
  <header className="sticky top-0 z-fixed">
    <div className="absolute z-dropdown">Dropdown</div>
  </header>
  <div className="z-modal-backdrop">Modal Backdrop</div>

Benefits:
  - Clear semantic meaning for each class
  - Obvious hierarchy and purpose
  - Easy to understand and maintain
  - Self-documenting code
